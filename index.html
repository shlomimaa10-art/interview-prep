How to use it
1
Get a question — paste your own or hit Generate to get one tailored to your level and focus areas.
2
Configure the session — set your level, style, focus areas, and AI provider.
3
Start the interview — the AI opens cold, no intro. Drive the design exactly like a real session.
4
Sketch on the whiteboard — click ✏️ Whiteboard to open the Excalidraw canvas. Draw shapes, arrows, and labels to sketch your architecture as you explain it. When the whiteboard has content it is automatically included with every message — the AI reads your diagram in real time.
5
Get feedback — type "feedback" at any point for a structured, honest evaluation of your answer.
Commands & shortcuts
"feedback"Stop and get a full structured evaluation — what you nailed, what you missed
"give full answer"Ask the AI to reveal the model solution
"hint"Get a nudge without giving the answer away
